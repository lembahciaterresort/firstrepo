The Ambience

Lembah Ciater Resort Managed by SAHID is a 3-stars resort that will allows you to eyewitness the fresh atmosphere combined with vintage touch of its various rooms. Stand right in front of Tangkuban Perahu Mountain, this resort also located near Sari Ater Hot Spring and only took 1 hour drive from Husan Sastranegara Airport Bandung. Offering 51 units in total with several unique type of rooms, Lembah Ciater Resort Managed by SAHID is the perfect choice that will suits your staycation needs.

Meeting & Events

Lembah Ciater Resort provides 3 meeting rooms with four options of table setting that would gladly afford any of your event necessity for up to 500 people in the warm and Java-authentic designed room which will create an ideal venue to every occasion.
